<span data-ttu-id="5009e-103">ポリモーフィズムは、カプセル化と継承に次ぐ、オブジェクト指向プログラミングの第 3 の柱と言われることがよくあります。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5009e-103">Polymorphism is often referred to as the third pillar of object-oriented programming, after encapsulation and inheritance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5009e-104">ポリモーフィズムは、ギリシャ語で "多形" を意味し、次の 2 つの側面を持っています。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5009e-104">Polymorphism is a Greek word that means "many-shaped" and it has two distinct aspects:</span></span>
